March 4, 2022 in Miami, FL

Because it was during the semester, I wasn't able to attend Dodie's show near my hometown in Michigan. My roommates and I were planning a trip to Miami, FL when one of them mentioned that they saw Dodie would be playing while we were there.

I have sweet memories of listening to Dodie all throughout high school, and it was incredible being able to finally see her live after all those years. Despite releasing lots of music beforehand, this tour was for Dodie's first full album.

Because of traffic, we didn't arrive until right when Lizzy McAlpine, the opener came on. I got an okay view of her in the back, but after Lizzy's set we ventured around the venue to find a better spot.

Dodie was incredible! They played many nostalgic fan favorites in a really cool mash-up on the piano. She also brought out Orla Gartland, Dodie's close friend and another artist I really enjoy. Together, they jammed out on the clarinet and drums during an instrumental break.

I loved all the arrangements Dodie and their band prepared. The Miami crowd made the experience loud and fun! It was my first time going to a concert outside of my hometown, and I would definitely do something like that again.
