april 23, 2022 in detroit, mi

I didn't know I would be attending the completely sold out SOUR Tour until about a month before my city's date. When tickets went on sale in December 2021, with Olivia Rodrigo being one of the biggest and newest names in pop music, her tickets sold out quicker than I could get them.

In March 2022, I was sent an email out of the blue telling me that there would be another opportunity to get tickets, and I received a pre-sale code. I ended up with 4 GA Balcony spots for my friends and I!

With SOUR being the soundtrack to my year, I was beyond excited to attend. Because both floor and balcony were GA in a venue that held around 5,000 people, we arrived pretty early at 11am and were within the first 100-200 people there.

We made friends with those in line around us, and took turns picking up lunch from the Detroit Filling Station nearby (yum!). Some cool things happened, like meeting Olivia's tour photographer and handing him a gift I made for her, and my friends and I got featured on Channel 95.5's Instagram story.

When we finally entered the venue, we managed to get front row in the section we were in! Below are some photos from the experience, you can click on them (in order) to see them up close :)

Overall, this was one of my favorites nights. The opener, Gracie Abrams, was incredible (another artist I adore), and Olivia sang her songs with new and super fun arrangements. The most memorable moment was 'jealousy, jealousy' the rock and roll version.

Here's the album version for reference :)

'jealousy, jealousy'

To finish out a great memory, Olivia posted about our particular show on Instagram. Definitely earned some bragging rights amongst other fans!
