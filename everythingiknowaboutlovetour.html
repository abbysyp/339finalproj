october 1, 2022 in ferndale, mi

I discovered Laufey (pronounced lay-vay) from a viral video about right when she started releasing music. Her music is at the intersection of jazz and pop, and I would mostly describe it as warm, cozy, and comforting.

Laufey is Icelandic and Chinese, so I was super happy to find Asian representation in her music. I found so much joy in watching her livestreams, where she would sing covers from some of my favorite jazz artists like Ella Fitzgerald. I brought my mom to this concert with me, as she grew to love these songs as much as I had.

The whole concert felt like a dream come true, and it was about the kindest crowd I've experienced. The venue was called The Loving Touch, and it was small and intimate (which I've discovered is my favorite type of venue). We only waited outside for an hour before the doors opened, and we were able to stand right in the front row!

I had a view of the backstage, so when the opener, Matilda Mann, was waiting for her cue to begin her set, we waved to each other :)

When Laufey came on stage, I couldn't believe how close we were. She performed fantastically, playing the piano, cello, and guitar throughout different songs! She talked to the crowd alot, too, and she was very sweet and funny.

I think the Everything I Know About Love Tour takes the #1 spot for me. I loved every second of it, and I can't wait to see Laufey again in the future!

Until then, I'll leave you with a collection of some of my favorite songs from her.
