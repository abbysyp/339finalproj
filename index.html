about me

Hi! My name is Abby, and I'm currently a student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying UX Design. As a final project for my accessible web design & development class this semester, I decided to highlight the different concert experiences I've had this year. I've never been to concerts before 2022, so I thought it would be fun to document the few I've seen.

I'm a huge fan of music, especially pop and jazz (which you'll soon find out as you explore this site)! I also enjoy crocheting, reading, and watching sitcoms in my free time.

Thanks for reading, and it's nice to meet you!
